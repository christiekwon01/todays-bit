# Daily Speaking Prompt Library

## How to use:
- Pick one question per day
- Give yourself 30 seconds to think
- Record 60-90 seconds of your response
- One take only — imperfection is progress

---

## CATEGORY 1: Personal Storytelling (builds narrative skills)

1. Tell me about a time you completely changed your mind about something important.
2. Describe a moment when you felt like an imposter.
3. What's a risk you took that didn't pay off? What did you learn?
4. Tell me about someone who influenced your career path without knowing it.
5. Describe a conversation that changed the trajectory of your life.
6. What's something you're terrible at but keep doing anyway?
7. Tell me about a time you had to apologize and really meant it.
8. Describe your most embarrassing professional moment.
9. What's a belief you held at 18 that you completely disagree with now?
10. Tell me about a time you stood up for something unpopular.

## Tips for choosing:

- **Mondays**: Start with storytelling or self-reflection
- **Midweek**: Try hot takes or professional prompts  
- **Fridays**: Go for creative/weird or rapid fire
- **Struggling?**: Pick from "Explain It Simply" — hard to mess up
- **Feeling confident?**: Challenge yourself with values & philosophy
- **Cultural commentary?**: Pick from Extended Culture & Society (prompts 161–260)

Remember: The goal isn't perfection. It's showing up. Your 100th recording will blow your mind compared to your first.
